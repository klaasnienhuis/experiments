How to geotag your models

  1. Zoom to a location on the map,
  2. Double-click on this location on the map,
  3. Tags for this location will be generated,
  4. Go to your model on Sketchfab,
  5. Edit the model properties,
  6. Add the generated tags to your model. You must add the required tags at least. Other optional tags are used to find nearby models.
  7. Once tagged, your models should show up on the map within the next hour.
